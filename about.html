About

The Founder

Viraj Singh

Viraj is a passion driven entrepreneur, speaker and a content creator. At the age of 15 he started his journey of content creation, after which there was no looking back.

Throughout the next few years, he developed his experience with over 500 content pieces created, managed 55+ marketing campaigns, and has put his expertise in entrepreneurship, marketing strategies and social media psychology in building up everything he’s been able to build so far.

At the age of 17 he dropped out of college and started WeeWin Media from scratch after knowing his passion for entrepreneurship and his deep desire to impact people. He also creates value adding content on Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn and Twitter.

He fundamentally believes in delivering work that will create impact and build long lasting relationships. He’s a learner with keen interest in Digital marketing, Social psychology, entrepreneurship, finance and life.
